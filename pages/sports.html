Our Department's Spirit

Our participation in this year's sports gala went beyond just playing on the field. We believe that unity is our greatest strength. Throughout the event, our department stood as a wall of support for our athletes. From organized cheering squads to providing moral encouragement during the high-pressure moments of the tug-of-war and football matches, our presence was felt in every corner of the stadium.

We raised our voices to ensure our participants never felt alone, proving that whether we win or lose, we do it together as a dedicated team.
